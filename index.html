Atharva

// Security Researcher · CTF Player · Content Creator

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast who breaks things to understand how they work. My focus is on web application security, network penetration testing, and CTF competitions — and I share everything I learn here and on YouTube.

When I'm not hunting for vulnerabilities or solving CTF challenges, I'm creating tutorials to help others get into the field. I believe security knowledge should be accessible to everyone.

My goal is to bridge the gap between complex security concepts and practical, real-world application — starting from zero, going to hero.
